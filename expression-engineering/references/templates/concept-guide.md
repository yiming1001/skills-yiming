# 认知解释型模板 / Concept Guide

Use when beginners or cross-functional readers need to understand a concept, method, model, product, or technical idea.

## Structure

1. What it is in one sentence.
2. Why the reader should care.
3. How it works.
4. Example or analogy close to the reader's work.
5. Common misunderstandings.
6. Where it applies and where it does not.
7. Short takeaway.

## Writing Rules

- Start from the reader's existing mental model.
- Keep examples concrete.
- Do not overload the first section with definitions.
- Use diagrams for mechanisms, flows, or boundaries.
