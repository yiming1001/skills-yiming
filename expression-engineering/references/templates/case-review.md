# 复盘案例型模板 / Case Review Guide

Use when summarizing a project, experiment, incident, campaign, or implementation case.

## Structure

1. Background.
2. Original goal.
3. Constraints and conflicts.
4. What was done.
5. Result.
6. Problems found.
7. Transferable lessons.
8. Next iteration.

## Writing Rules

- Keep the messy middle; do not make the case sound like marketing.
- Include what did not work.
- Distinguish result from lesson.
- Keep evidence, caveats, and unfinished risks visible.
