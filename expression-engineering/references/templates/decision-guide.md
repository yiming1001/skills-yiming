# 决策型模板 / Decision Guide

Use when leaders, product owners, project managers, clients, or reviewers need to choose a direction.

## Structure

1. Decision to make.
2. Current situation and pressure.
3. Options compared.
4. Recommendation.
5. Cost, benefit, risk, and dependencies.
6. What needs approval.
7. Next action and owner.

## Writing Rules

- Put the recommendation early.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and judgment.
- Use comparison tables, decision matrices, or risk matrices.
- Do not hide unresolved questions. Put them near the approval point.
