# 管理规范型模板 / Policy Or SOP Guide

Use when the audience needs repeatable standards, governance, quality rules, review criteria, or team process.

## Structure

1. Purpose and scope.
2. Applicable and non-applicable scenarios.
3. Roles and responsibilities.
4. Standard process.
5. Required inputs and outputs.
6. Quality criteria.
7. Exceptions and escalation.
8. Review cadence.

## Writing Rules

- Define boundaries clearly.
- Use checklists for review criteria.
- Avoid ceremonial wording; make rules observable.
- Include exception handling and escalation paths.
