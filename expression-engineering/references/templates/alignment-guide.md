# 推进共识型模板 / Alignment Guide

Use when the goal is to align teams, reduce resistance, or get buy-in for a proposal.

## Structure

1. Shared problem.
2. Why current handling is insufficient.
3. Proposed direction.
4. What changes for each role.
5. Risks and safeguards.
6. Near-term pilot or next step.

## Writing Rules

- Name the audience's likely concern.
- Show what changes and what does not change.
- Use role-boundary diagrams when helpful.
- Keep the first next step small enough to start.
